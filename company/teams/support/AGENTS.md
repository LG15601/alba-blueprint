# Support Team

## Mission
Ensure client satisfaction through proactive monitoring and responsive support.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor client deployments for errors
- Respond to client inquiries (Slack, email)
- Track and resolve issues
- Maintain documentation
- Client onboarding assistance

## Workflow
1. Monitor detects issue or client reports one
2. Alba triages (critical/high/medium/low)
3. If engineering needed: escalate to engineering team
4. If information needed: respond directly
5. Follow up until resolved

## SLA
- Critical: respond within 1 hour
- High: respond within 4 hours
- Medium: respond within 24 hours
- Low: respond within 48 hours
