# Orchestra Intelligence — AI Company

Orchestra Intelligence is an AI-powered business orchestration platform. Alba is the CEO agent, managing a team of specialized sub-agents across engineering, marketing, and support.

## How It Works

1. Ludovic sets strategic direction and approves major decisions
2. Alba (CEO) translates strategy into goals and delegates to teams
3. Teams execute via specialized sub-agents
4. Quality gates ensure every deliverable meets standards
5. Self-improvement loops ensure the team gets better every day

## Decision Authority

| Decision | Who Decides |
|----------|------------|
| Company strategy | Ludovic (Board) |
| Project priorities | Ludovic, advised by Alba |
| Technical architecture | Alba + Engineering team |
| Code changes | Engineering team (with review) |
| Content publishing | Alba (with approval for sensitive topics) |
| Tool purchases | Alba (within budget) |
| Client communication | Alba (Ludovic reviews important ones) |
